We do not require users to create an account. We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable information directly.

The App may collect certain information automatically to improve stability, performance, and user experience, including:

This data is collected through third-party services listed below.

The App may display advertisements. Advertising partners may collect:

These services operate under their own privacy policies:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

We do not sell, trade, or rent users’ personal information. Data may be shared:

We retain collected data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law.

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.
